01 Executive Summary

Developed a high-performance web application designed to automate logistics processes within the Market Research industry. The tool addresses the challenge of processing thousands of survey response rows (Top-up data) for reward distribution (e-vouchers) and managing costs for sample provider partners (Vendors).

02 The Problem

  • • Time-Consuming: Staff spent hours filtering and splitting Excel files for each project.
  • • Risk of Errors: Manual copy-pasting prone to errors in incentive amounts or recipient information.
  • • Lack of Transparency: Difficulties in tracking actual vs. projected budgets, especially with multiple international vendors.

03 The Solution

  • • Automated Splitting: Automatically splits files by Vendor/Referrer using drag-and-drop.
  • • Real-time Cost Engine: Intelligent cost calculation supporting USD/VND and service fees.
  • • Budget Forecasting: Real-time cumulative cost estimation for optimized sample allocation.
